WHO ARE WE?

Tipsy Treefrog is a family-owned craft brewery in the sunny state of Florida. We specialize in unique, small-batch sours, but you're sure to find something that everyone enjoys!

FEATURED BEER

TART GREEN TREEFROG

Our flagship sour brew that is tart and juicy throughout. Brewed with refreshing citrus, peach, and passionfruit flavors.

LIGHT HOPS LEOPARD

Our IPA will have you HOPPING back to our pond for more. Perfect for casual sipping

TOAD-ALLY TOASTED

The only thing more short and STOUT than an American Toad! Notes of roasted nuts and toasted coffee make up this dark brew.

DAZZLING DART FROGS

Double the dose of pucker-inducing goodness! Fermented twice and filled with real raspberries and blackberries - sure to satisfy sour lovers.
